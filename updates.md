02 — A note from Isaac (WHY) (add my picture) inspo:  
Hi, my name is Isaac. 
When I was starting out my digital career, I was that person, spending hours 
online, going through programs, trying to find the right skills to open doors as a 
young African. The content was out there. But most of it was built for someone 
else. The creators were speaking to a different reality, and it was hard to connect 
with what they were saying. 
And the few local options? Most of them were just business. No real community. 
No ongoing support. Just a course, sometimes outdated, and then nothing. The 
path felt lonely. 
So I'm building what I wished I had starting out. 
At Creative Saako, every coach is someone actively doing the work right now, 
not someone who studied it years ago and forgot what starting out feels like. The 
skills are practical, not theoretical. And around you the whole time is a real 
community, people on the same path, during the program and long after it ends. 
That is what builds a successful digital career. 
— Isaac 
Link to his website: https://www.evanbryceriddle.com/
